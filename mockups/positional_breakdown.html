Option B: Deviation Bars

Separate section showing +/− vs position average
A standalone card where each bar is centered on the position average. Bars extend right (green) when above average or left (red) when below. This is more prominent and would work as a collapsible sub-section beneath the existing percentile bars. More visual weight, but immediately shows strengths/weaknesses at a glance.
